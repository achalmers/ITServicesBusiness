About NexaTech Solutions

We're on a mission to give every startup access to the same quality of IT support that Fortune 500 companies take for granted — at a price that makes sense for growing businesses.

Our Story

Built for the Businesses That Get Overlooked

NexaTech Solutions was founded with a simple observation: small businesses and startups are constantly underserved by the IT industry. Large managed service providers focus on enterprise clients. Break-fix shops are reactive and expensive. And hiring a full-time IT person is out of reach for most 10-person teams.

We saw founders spending hours troubleshooting WiFi issues, onboarding employees without proper security controls, and losing sleep over backups that may or may not be working. These teams had real ambition — they just didn't have the IT infrastructure to match it.

So we built NexaTech Solutions: a managed IT service designed from the ground up for startups. Flat-rate pricing. No long contracts. A real human who knows your systems and picks up the phone. The kind of IT partner that helps you move fast and stay secure, so you can focus on building your business.

Our Focus

Why We Specialize in Startups & Small Teams

Most IT companies go after the big fish — enterprise contracts with hundreds of employees and five-figure monthly retainers. We've made a deliberate choice to focus on the segment that needs it most: growing businesses with 5–50 employees.

Startups face a unique set of challenges. You're moving fast, often onboarding employees without proper IT controls. Your team might be remote, hybrid, or spinning up a new office. You're dealing with investor due diligence questions about your security posture. And you need it all handled now.

We've built our service specifically around this context. Our tooling, our processes, and our pricing are all calibrated for the startup lifecycle — from Series A through your first 50 hires.
