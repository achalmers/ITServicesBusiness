Network & Infrastructure Setup

A reliable, fast, and secure network is the backbone of your business. We design and deploy professional network infrastructure tailored to your office layout and team size — the kind usually reserved for companies 10x your size.

  • Enterprise WiFi deployment (access points, SSIDs)
  • Structured cabling and patch panel installation
  • Managed switches and VLAN configuration
  • Router setup and WAN failover
  • Network performance monitoring
  • Guest network isolation and segmentation
  • VoIP phone system integration
  • Ongoing maintenance and firmware updates
👥 For: New offices, relocations, and teams with unreliable connectivity

  • Cloud-to-cloud backup (Microsoft 365 data)
  • Backup monitoring and health alerts
  • Documented disaster recovery plan
  • Recovery time objective (RTO) planning
  • Annual recovery testing and drills
  • Ransomware-resistant immutable backups
👥 For: Any business that can't afford to lose critical data or suffer extended downtime

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started with NexaTech Solutions.

Do I need to sign a long-term contract? +

No. All NexaTech plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment required. We believe in earning your business every single month through quality service — not by locking you in. You can cancel with 30 days' notice at any time.

How fast is your response time when something breaks? +

We guarantee same-day response on all support tickets during business hours (Mon–Fri, 8am–6pm ET). For critical issues affecting your entire team, we typically respond within 1–2 hours. Growth and Enterprise clients receive priority queue access for even faster response.

Do you work with fully remote teams? +

Absolutely. Many of our clients operate fully remote or hybrid. The majority of IT support — password resets, software issues, security incidents, cloud configuration — can be handled entirely remotely. For clients who need physical hardware support, we coordinate with local technicians or ship pre-configured equipment directly to employees.

What geographic areas do you serve? +

We provide remote support to businesses nationwide across the United States. On-site support and regular visits are available in the greater upstate New York region. For clients outside this area who need on-site work, we can coordinate with local IT vendors in your market.

How do I get started with NexaTech Solutions? +

Easy! Fill out our contact form or give us a call at (607) 765-5410. We'll schedule a free 30-minute consultation to understand your current setup and business needs. From there, we'll recommend the right plan and can typically begin onboarding within one week of signing up.
