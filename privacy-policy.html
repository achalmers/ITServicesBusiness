Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

NexaTech Solutions ("NexaTech," "we," "us," or "our") provides managed IT services to small businesses and startups. This policy explains what information we collect through our website and customer portal, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Contact form submissions — name, company, email, phone number, service interest, and message content.
  • Customer portal accounts — name, email, phone, company, and a securely hashed password.
  • Support tickets — subject, description, category, priority, any file attachments, and the resulting communication thread with our team.
  • Automatically collected data — IP address (used for basic rate-limiting and spam prevention on our contact form) and standard web server logs.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide the IT services you request.
  • To manage your support tickets and notify you of updates by email.
  • To send you an auto-reply confirming receipt of a contact form submission or ticket.
  • To maintain the security of our systems, including detecting abuse of our contact form.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Cookies & Sessions

Our customer portal and admin systems use a session cookie strictly to keep you logged in. Session cookies are set with HttpOnly, Secure, and SameSite=Strict flags and expire automatically. We do not use advertising cookies.

Our public website pages use Google Analytics 4 to understand traffic and improve our site, which sets its own cookies to distinguish visitors. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, or by using your browser's tracking-prevention settings.

Third-Party Services

We use a small number of third-party services to operate our site and business:

  • Google Analytics — to measure website traffic and see which pages/marketing channels are effective. Google's own privacy practices are described in their Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — for site typography.
  • Our email/SMTP provider — to deliver ticket notifications and contact form replies.
  • Our web hosting provider — to store and serve website and account data.

These providers only receive the minimum information needed to perform their function and are not authorized to use it for any other purpose.

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions, ticket history, and account information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, maintain support history, and meet legal/accounting obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see below).

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us using the details below. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date above.
